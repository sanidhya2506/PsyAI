The Heart Behind PsyAI

I built PsyAI because mental health support shouldn't be a luxury. In a world that never sleeps, many of us carry burdens in silence because of the stigma, the cost, or simply because it's "not the right time" for an appointment.

PsyAI was born from a simple belief: Everyone deserves to be heard, immediately and without judgment. This isn't just code; it's a bridge for those moments when you need a gentle mirror for your thoughts.

The PsyAI Approach

We combine advanced intelligence with human-centric empathy to support your growth.

Total Anonymity

No accounts, no tracking. Your data is your own. We process your words and forget them as soon as you leave the session.

Deep Empathy

Powered by Gemini, our AI doesn't just respond; it listens and reflects your emotions with genuine care and patience.

Coping Strategies

Identify negative patterns and explore gentle, science-backed ways to manage stress, anxiety, or mental burnout.

Judgment Free

Speak your truth without fear. PsyAI provides a neutral ground where every thought is valid and every feeling is heard.

24/7 Availability

Thoughts don't wait for office hours. Whether it is 3 AM or mid-day, our sanctuary is always open for you.

Self-Reflection

Use our tools to mirror your thoughts back to you, helping you find answers that were already inside you.

Emotional Literacy

Beyond just venting, PsyAI helps you put names to complex feelings. Developing this vocabulary is the first step toward mastering your emotional well-being.

Zero Barrier Entry

No waiting rooms, no insurance forms, and no "nerves" about meeting someone new. Just a clean, quiet space to start healing at your own pace.

Questions & Clarity

Is my data used for training? +
No. Your privacy is our priority. We use the Gemini API in a way that your personal conversations are not used to train the model.
Can I use this for medical diagnosis? +
Absolutely not. PsyAI is for emotional support only. Please consult a psychiatrist or clinical psychologist for diagnosis.
How much does it cost? +
PsyAI is currently free to use as part of our mission to make mental support accessible to everyone.
